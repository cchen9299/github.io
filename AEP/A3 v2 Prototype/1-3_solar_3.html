AEP has proposed to produce approximately 3,400 megawatts of solar power by 2034. In 2016, the company continued to work toward that goal by opening four new solar power stations within the Indiana Michigan system: Deer Creek, Twin Branch, Olive and Watervliet. Together, they produce nearly 15 megawatts of power.


These solar stations enable Indiana Michigan to evaluate the performance of various types of solar panels across its service area.


AEP Ohio and Appalachian Power have plans to build and operate their own solar facilities in 2018 and 2019, respectively.


AEP also purchases and distributes solar power through purchase agreements with other developers and operators.
