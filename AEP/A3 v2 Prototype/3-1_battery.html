Innovations in Battery Storage

How do we effectively store electricity once it’s been generated? The obvious answer: batteries. But a battery that can fulfill the needs of a sophisticated power system is a lot more complicated than what you put in your flashlight.


The industry needs batteries that can:

•     Provide backup power in case of an outage

•     Be compatible with and support wind and solar energy production

•     Provide additional power during peak demand

•     Regulate the flow of power so it can be used by sensitive electronics

•     Respond to frequency regulation signals on the power grid


AEP is an industry leader in the ongoing development of batteries that can address these and other customer needs. The company is continuing to pursue strategic partnerships that create cost-effective, efficient and reliable energy storage.
