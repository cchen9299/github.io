AEP Transmission: Legacy & Vision

Legacy & Vision provides a look at AEP Transmission’s long history of innovation and service, as well as examining its leadership role in today’s competitive electric-transmission market.  The 7:26-minute video has an original music score and was produced by AEP in collaboration with film students at the Columbus College of Art and Design during the 2013-14 school year. The film has two parts entitled Legacy and Vision.
