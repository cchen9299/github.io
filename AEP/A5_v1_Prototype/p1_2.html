In 2006, AEP released its first Corporate Accountability Report.


This report is different from a traditional annual report. It allows the company to highlight its corporate culture, innovations and social responsibility initiatives, along with its financial performance and business strategy. Employees, shareholders, potential investors, customers, elected officials and the public can also learn more about the ways in which AEP is present in their communities.


The report outlines AEP’s long- and short-term goals as well as its successes. Of equal importance, the report identifies areas in which AEP can do better. The digital edition contains links, videos and a search function that makes it easy for readers to find specific information.


As the world and the industry evolve, AEP’s challenges and goals evolve, as well. The Corporate Accountability Report provides a window into AEP’s developing mission as a corporate citizen.
