Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI) uses smart meters that are tied to communication systems in order to connect utility companies with their customers. AMI technologies enable customers to see their meter readings in real time on their smart devices. Customers can then use this information to adjust their consumption, save money and conserve energy.


AEP has installed nearly 2 million smart meters, with even more on the way.
