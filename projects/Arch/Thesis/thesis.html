Architecture Undergraduate Thesis

This thesis is not a study of the anatomy of architecture or its physical form. It does not pose a question of tectonics nor does it study the materiality of buildings.

Instead, this thesis seeks to capture and transcribe the experience of the spaces that are often unacknowledged. It is a study of the chemistry of an architectural space as it is created through three stages of drawings: the Twin Structures, the Connections and the Memories.
