Clerks of Works:
Warehouse

Spring 2014

Project Breif

The Clerks of Works Warehouse is part of the Clerks of Works complex designed to facilitate needs of the test cell reserch labs (RDF) in Blacksburg. Inspired by Mies’ Crown Hall at IIT, I chose to do a grand uninturrupted room using double tees to strengthen the idea of this grand hall. The linear strips of skylight that dropps to the ground creates a transition upon entering the building as well as eliminating corners that will help me avoid creating a space only of function
