My name is Hank Chen and I am a recent graduate from Virginia Tech with a Bachelor's of Architecture in Architecture. I am currently finishing up my web development program at Bloc.

So I went and explore a lot of different things when I was in school including photography, drawing, printmaking, industrial design, toy making, urban and sustainable planning... and I picked up coding somewhere along the way on my own. I never really obtained the level of skill I would like to have in coding in school. So I signed up for an online class while working for a construction firm after graduation. Here I am after 35 weeks of web development course looking for opportunities in the field!
